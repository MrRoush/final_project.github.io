Our Animatronic Characters

Freddy Fazbear

Freddy Fazbear is the lead singer of the animatronic band at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. He is known for his friendly demeanor and love for entertaining children.

Bonnie the Bunny

Bonnie the Bunny is the guitarist of the band. He is energetic and always ready to put on a great show for the guests.

Chica the Chicken

Chica the Chicken is the backup vocalist and loves to dance. She enjoys making new friends and spreading joy.

Foxy the Pirate Fox

Foxy the Pirate Fox is known for his adventurous spirit. He often entertains guests with his pirate-themed antics.
